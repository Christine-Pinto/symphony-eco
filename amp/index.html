Who We Are

Who We Are

We are a Canadian and a German who met as Volunteer leaders at a Non-Profit Organization.

We have traveled through almost 100 countries, volunteered at well over 50 organizations, and have passionately grown our knowledge about environmental conservation and Volunteerism with each new country.

We are growing a global community of Activists, including NPOs, Governments, and Individuals who will protect our vulnerable planet.

What We Know

What We Know

We share an extremely valuable set of experience, expertise, and education.

We are professional NPO Board Members, Technologists, and Project Managers. We have degrees and certifications in Environmental Sciences, Volunteer Leadership, Business, Technology, and Project Management.

We have years of experience in managing Volunteer-Driven Non-Profit Organizations and Government Agencies using Technology, Marketing, and Fundraising.

Why We Help

Why We Help

The short answer: because we just can not stand by when the environment is in such peril.

We have seen, so many times now, that NGOs and Governments have determination but always find themselves limited by the exact same issues: People, Brand Awareness, and Money.

This is where we come in. Our experience and training has given us the tools to address all of these issues together. And, without a doubt, it all starts with Volunteers.
